## What is is it
Ever had a problem learning your new favorite opening but you encounter this two problems.
1. Your opponent plays the wrong moves
2. Theory and memorizing is not helping

Then I present you **Learn Your Opening**, an app idea that helps people learn their favorite opening with experience. Not by learn but by doing.

## How does it work
Just pick the opening/s you want to learn and you will match up with someone that wants to learn defending **YOUR** opening of choice.

### Move Opening Helper
Think of **MOH** as a babysitter that helps you play the first moves together. Same thing happens with your opponent as well.

#### How much does MOH really do
Honestly... you pick. Do you want to learn the Sicilian defense? MOH assists you by visualizing together the 3 theory moves of the Sicilian. Wanna learn the Dragon more moves etc. After you gain some experience and the hang of it you can turn MOH off, but remember. You **<font color="#ff0000">must</font>**
play the correct moves, or MOH wakes up and tells you that you were wrong. Of course it is polite and guides you on what went wrong, and corrects you.

### The Game
After **MOH** assists you, this is it. You can now start playing and learning your favorite opening.

### Play with friends
You can easily link up with friends and play whatever opening you want

## The Big Problem
While this is a decent idea it has one major issue.
In order to play games you need <font color="#ff0000">people</font>. You need users that want to play from the Caro Kann defence to the Norwagean Sunfish. This particular idea would fit perfectly into an already big app like **Chess.com** or **Lichess** as an extension.
